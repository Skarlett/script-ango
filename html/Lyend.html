V'anrod

V'anrod (Vhey-an-rode) , The blade used by Lyend, Vi Victorian. The blade Gathers it name by the first who made it, V'si (Vhey-sigh), a master who died earily on in making blades. V'anrod was his blade of perfence in all of his battles, but in a battle with him and a Demon named Worm, he had been severely injured. Slowly dying, he had given the sword off to a corrupt soldier who only desired power, using the blade as a way to gain power. Longer on this soldier became the leader of his cult known as "Cyther" (Si-ther), and all who were in the group were given a small portion of the blade's power. Lyend being part of this cult, he slowly advanced in the ranks of the 9 users. The sword was enchanted with the powers of Shadow and hate, and the Demon who's soul was invested in the Blade was known as Divinarex. She had found a strange hate in Lyend and soon terrorized him all the way until he had assassinated the leader, Cyther. Claiming the blade as his own, she had taken over his soul with her own, he had killed off all the Cythers using the blades power reclaiming the power that had perviously belonged to the blade. Lyend had blacked out fro many years allowing Divinarex complete power. One day he decided to rebel within his own self conscience. Winning the fight between the two, he added a chain to the blade in which he synced Divinarex's soul in. This gave him the choice to choose how much power he wanted from the blade but the trade off of how much power he gave her to his body.
